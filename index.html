About Me

Welcome to my portfolio! My name is Quinton Mikula and I graduated from the
University of South Florida in 2021 with a Bachelor's of Science in Business
Administration. I am an outgoing and active person that loves to learn new things.
In my free time, I enjoy working out at the gym, and hanging out with friends or
family. I find it very enjoyable to overcome challenges and solve problems.

All my life, I've been told that I am a very analytical person. I like to do research
and sift through data in order to discover new things. While in college, I took
a class called Market Research, and it had us utilizing datasets and creating
visuals to figure out what the data was telling us. It was one of my favorite classes,
and really got me thinking about careers in this field. Also, Programming languages
have always been fascinating to me and I wanted to challenge myself to learn at least one.

I would like to pursue a career path in Data Analytics as this is what I'm passionate
about. I am very dedicated to my work and driven. When I work on a new project, I like
to see it through until the end. I love visualizing data so that I can discover hidden
insights and trends, and create simple visuals that easily tell the story to management,
clients, or stakeholders. I am very adaptable, and I know I can provide beneficial work to
any company. Life is too short to not challenge yourself and become your absolute best.
