About Me

Welcome to my portfolio! My name is Quinton Mikula and I graduated from the
University of South Florida in 2021 with a Bachelor's of Science in Business
Administration. I am an outgoing and active person that loves to learn new things.
In my free time, I enjoy working out at the gym, and hanging out with friends or
family. I also love challenges and basically anything that involves problem-solving.

I've been told that I am a very analytical person all my life. I like to do research
and sift through data in order to find out new things. While in college, I took
a class called Market Research, and it had us utilizing datasets and creating
visuals to find out what the data could tell us. It was one of my favorite classes,
and really got me thinking about careers in this field. Also, Programming languages
have always been fascinating to me and I wanted to challenge myself to learn a few.

I would like to pursue a career path in Data Analysis as this is what I'm passionate
about. I am very dedicated to my work and driven. When I work on a new project, I like
to see it through until the end. I love visualizing data so that I can find out what
the data is telling us, and create simple visuals that easily paint the picture to others
in the company. I believe that I could easily adapt and provide beneficial work to a
company. Life is too short to not enjoy every second and to not challenge yourself.
